General Rules

  1. Our CTF competition encourages individual participation and no team registration are available.
  2. When the CTF event starts, Users can download the challenges in our website and submit the flag via our telegram bot @TamilCTF_Bot only.
  3. Telegram Bot will be active during the CTF event only.
  4. Bruteforcing our API,Webservers,Telegram Bot is strictly prohibited,IP ban will be processed if done.
  5. Do not share flags to spoil the thrill of the CTF event.
  6. If you find any difficulties or issues while solving challenges, contact admins.

    Join our Telegram group

Flag Submission

  1. Connect to the telegram bot @TamilCTF_Bot
  2. Send /name {your_registerationname} to register your points and display it in the scoreboard.
    This username will be used to refer you for this whole event.
  3. Choose your username wisely, because once registered you cannot change your name during event.
  4. Only alphanumeric characters are allowed for username.
    Try to use unique username for better reach.
  5. To submit the flag, /flag TamilCTF{flag_here}.
  6. If the flag is correct, you will be given points and it'll be updated in our scoreboard.
  7. Once correct flag is submitted.You cannot submit it again, as it becomes "Incorrect".
  8. Don't try to spam with the bot.

DON'T BRUTEFORCE,DON'T SHARE FLAGS
